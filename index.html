About Me

I am a researcher with a strong interest in spatial-temporal data mining, leveraging techniques such as deep learning and topological data analysis, with a particular focus on urban informatics.

With a background in mathematical science, I strive to bridge the gap between theoretical research and practical applications. I'm always eager to learn new technologies and collaborate on innovative projects.
